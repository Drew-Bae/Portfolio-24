Code Career Guide

In shaping the design of Code Career Guide, a support app tailored for recent computer science graduates, I embraced a user-focused design approach.


The crux of my methodology relied on extracting insights from usability studies, which encompassed interviews, unmoderated usability studies, and additional data sources.


Over the course of the entire 4-week duration, I was responsible for the end-to-end UX of this Capstone project, ensuring a holistic and user-centric approach for the application.
